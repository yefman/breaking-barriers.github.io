Concept

"Breaking Barriers" is an interactive installation that aims to inspire community action and shine a light on the barriers faced by marginalized people on a daily basis. It urges us to recognize the barriers that exist in our society and take steps towards dismantling them. This installation takes the form of a brick wall, with visitors invited to throw heart-shaped objects (love) at the individual bricks, chipping away at the obstacles holding back progress and equality.

By physically (and philosophically) breaking down these barriers, visitors can contribute to a collective solution that builds a more equitable future for marginalized people. As each brick falls, an opportunity for a more peaceful and loving future arises, filled with positivity and hope. The experience is further enhanced by a series of video presentations that reimagine famous speeches and songs conveying messages of peace, equality, and optimism.

"Breaking Barriers" is a call to action and a reminder that change starts with us. Through the universal language of art, we aim to connect people from all walks of life and encourage a more open-minded and empathetic world view. Join us in this mission to break down barriers and make a positive impact on the world.
