How to Play

Pick up the power of love from the Love Bin.

Take your place at the line of equality (yellow line), poised and ready to make a difference. Direct your aim towards the systemic wall, brimming with barriers, and unleash the power of love.

Approach the wall and try on the solidarity lenses. Touch any of the silver bricks and peer through the wall's holes for a glimpse into a barrierless reality.
